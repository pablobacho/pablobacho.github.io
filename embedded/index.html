Embedded

My name is Pablo, and I help businesses build solutions based on embedded systems.

As an electronics engineer and an embedded systems specialist I can help you design, validate, develop, prototype and manufacture your product:

  • Meetings with your team in the early stages of conceptualization of a new product, providing my experience on electronics design, test and the manufacturing industry.
  • Helping you choose the best key components –Bill-of-Materials (BOM)– for your application.
  • Designing your electronics and electrical circuits.
  • Developing firmware for your microcontroller(s).
  • Prototyping the electronic circuits for your product.
  • Helping you set up electronics manufacturing plans.
  • As a reviewer in every stage of the process: BOM, design and code review.

I can help you in every step of your project, or I can support specific stages of development of your product.

I am available for remote-work worldwide. My timezone is Europe/Madrid.

Remote temperature monitoring for refrigerators

Lighting control over the Internet

Firmware development of a light source for endoscopies
